About Us

Who We Are

LFC First Church is the first church ever of the Living Faith Church Worldwide — also known as Winners' Chapel — a Spirit-filled, Word-based ministry born out of a divine mandate to liberate the world from every oppression of the devil through the preaching of the Word of Faith.

Our Story

On May 2, 1981, Bishop David Oyedepo received a life-changing 18-hour vision from God in Ilesa, Osun State, Nigeria. In that encounter, God commissioned him with the words:

"Now the hour has come to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil, through the preaching of the Word of Faith; and I am sending you to undertake this task."

What began as a small fellowship of four members in Kaduna in 1983 has grown into one of the largest Christian congregations on earth — with over 6 million members across 147 nations, anchored by the 50,000-seat Faith Tabernacle at Canaanland, Ota, Nigeria.

LFC First Church stands as a local expression of this global mandate — a community where the Word of Faith is preached, lives are restored, and destinies are fulfilled.

Our Spiritual Father

Bishop David Oyedepo

Founder and Presiding Bishop of Living Faith Church Worldwide — a man whose obedience to a divine mandate has transformed millions of lives across the globe.

Bishop David O. Oyedepo

Presiding Bishop · LFCWW
1981 Founded
147+ Nations
100+ Books

"Now the hour has come to liberate the world from all oppressions of the devil, through the preaching of the Word of Faith; and I am sending you to undertake this task."

— God's mandate to Bishop Oyedepo, May 2, 1981

Bishop David Olaniyi Oyedepo was born on September 27, 1954 in Osogbo, Osun State, Nigeria. He gave his life to Jesus Christ in 1969 through the influence of his teacher, Ms. Betty Lasher. He studied Architecture at Kwara State Polytechnic, Ilorin — the very city where LFC First Church stands today — before resigning from the Federal Ministry of Housing to answer the call of God full-time.

On May 2, 1981, at age 26, Bishop Oyedepo received an extraordinary 18-hour vision from God in Ilesa, Osun State, commissioning him to liberate the world from every oppression of the devil through the preaching of the Word of Faith. This divine encounter birthed what is today known as Living Faith Church Worldwide (Winners' Chapel).

The church started with just four members in Kaduna in December 1983, was officially commissioned by Pastor E.A. Adeboye of RCCG, and has since grown into one of the largest Christian congregations on earth — with over 6 million members across 147 nations, anchored by the 50,400-seat Faith Tabernacle at Canaanland, Ota — built debt-free within 12 months.
